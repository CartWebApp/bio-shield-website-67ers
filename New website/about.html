About BioShield

Our Story

BioShield was founded with a simple mission: to create a natural bug repellent that protects families without harmful chemicals. Inspired by the great outdoors, we developed formulas that are safe, effective, and environmentally responsible.

Our Ingredients & Safety

Our products are made with carefully selected natural ingredients, free from harsh chemicals and DEET. We believe in safe solutions that protect your skin and your family without compromise.

Sustainability & Manufacturing

BioShield products are proudly made in the USA using environmentally friendly packaging. We are committed to reducing waste and minimizing our carbon footprint while delivering high-quality repellents.
